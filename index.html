❓ FAQ

What is Nisab?

Minimum wealth threshold: 87.48g gold or 612.36g silver. Most scholars use the lower (silver) for inclusivity.

1 Tola = ? grams

1 Tola = 11.6638 grams (South Asian unit). Our calculator handles conversion automatically.
