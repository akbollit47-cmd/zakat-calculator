❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Nisab in Zakat?

Nisab is the minimum wealth threshold for Zakat obligation. For gold: 87.48 grams. For silver: 612.36 grams. Most scholars recommend using the lower threshold (silver) to benefit more people.

How to calculate Zakat on gold?

Zakat on gold is 2.5% of the total value. Formula: Weight (grams) × Purity % × Current Rate per gram × 2.5%. Ensure you've held the gold for one lunar year (Hawl) and it exceeds Nisab.

What is the difference between Zakat and Ushr?

Zakat (2.5%) is due on savings, gold, silver, and business assets held for one lunar year. Ushr (5% or 10%) is due on agricultural produce at harvest time—10% for rain-fed crops, 5% for irrigated crops.
