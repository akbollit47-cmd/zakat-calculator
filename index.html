📖 About This Calculator

Helping Muslims calculate Zakat with ease & accuracy

☽ What is This Tool?

The Zakat Calculator 2026 is a free, comprehensive Islamic financial tool designed to help Muslims worldwide accurately calculate their annual Zakat obligation. It covers all major zakatable assets — gold (with karat purity), silver (with purity), cash, business inventory, agricultural produce, and livestock.

Built with live metal prices and real-time exchange rates, the calculator supports 56+ Muslim-majority countries, automatically converting Nisab thresholds into your local currency.

✅ 2026 New Features

  • 🥇 Gold Karat system: 24K (100%), 22K (91.6%), 21K (87.5%), 18K (75%)
  • 🥈 Silver Purity system: 999 (100%), 925 (92.5%), 900 (90%), Custom %
  • ⚖️ Gram ↔ Tola conversion fixed (1 Tola = 11.6638g, not 1g)
  • 🌟 Total Wealth Zakat: combined assets vs gold Nisab
  • 📡 Improved live API with Live/Fallback status badge
  • 🌙 Dark mode + 15+ language support
  • 💾 Auto-save with localStorage
  • 📱 PWA installable for offline use

⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is for guidance and educational purposes. Zakat rulings can vary by Madhab and individual circumstances. For binding rulings, always consult a qualified Islamic scholar (Aalim/Mufti).

❓ FAQ

What is Nisab?

Minimum wealth threshold: 87.48g of gold or 612.36g of silver. The lower is used (usually silver).

Why are Gram and Tola different?

1 Tola = 11.6638 grams. Entering "1" in tola mode means 11.66 grams — not 1 gram. This was a bug in older versions.

What does "Total Wealth Zakat" mean?

If your combined assets (gold + silver + cash + business) exceed the Gold Nisab (87.48g gold value), Zakat is calculated at 2.5% on the total — giving you one combined number.

Live vs Fallback rates?

If internet is available, live gold/silver prices are fetched. Otherwise, reliable 2026 estimates are used. The status badge shows which is active.
